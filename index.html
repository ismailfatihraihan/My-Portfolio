While managing my studies and an engineering internship, I approached learning Japanese like an engineering problem: setting measurable goals and using spaced repetition. Within 5 months, I reached N4 level, and I am now preparing for the JLPT N3 exam this July.

I believe my structured approach to learning and deep care for end-users align perfectly with Japanese engineering teams. The culture of craftsmanship (ものづくり) and kaizen (改善) resonates deeply with my work ethic, and I am eager to grow within it.
